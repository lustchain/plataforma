Bridge protection

Practical routing

Auto check

Before burning LUSDT, the page checks whether the destination lockbox currently has enough USDT. This helps users avoid burning to a network with low available liquidity.

Users can buy on Polygon and sell on BSC only when that BSC reserve is available.
Step 1

Deposit from Polygon or BSC

The lockbox holds USDT. After confirmations, the bridge signs a claim so you can mint LUSDT on LUST Chain.

Bridge fee0.002000 LUSDT
You receive0.998000 LUSDT
Deposit flow
1Approve USDT to the selected lockbox.
2Deposit USDT. The watcher waits confirmations and creates a claim.
3The page auto-checks the API. When ready, switch to LUST Chain and mint LUSDT.
