Reward behavior
- No public miner active: official emergency producers keep the network running and receive 2 LST/block.
- One public miner active: that public producer receives 2 LST/block.
- Two or more public miners active: block producer receives 1.4 LST and other active public miners divide 0.6 LST.
- Official bootstrap producers are excluded from the public 30% committee share while public miners are active.
- Priority fees go to the real block producer; base fee is burned by EIP-1559.
