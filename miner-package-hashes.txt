Reward rules V31H2I:
- No public miners: official producers keep the chain alive and receive 2 LST.
- 1 public miner: public miner has first deterministic chance. If official fallback produces, official receives 1.4 LST and the active public miner receives 0.6 LST.
- 2+ public miners: block producer receives 1.4 LST and the other active public miners share 0.6 LST.

Chance rule:
- 1 registered active wallet = 1 chance.
- CPU/GPU power does not increase chance.
- Never enter seed phrase or private key.
- Use only the public 0x wallet address.
