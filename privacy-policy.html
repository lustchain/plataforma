Overview

LUST Chain is an experimental, community-driven blockchain project. This Privacy Policy explains how LUST Chain and its related websites and services — including lustchain.org, explorer.lustchain.org, rpc.lustchain.org, rpc-backup.lustchain.org, and platform.lustchain.org (collectively, the “Services”) — collect, use, and protect information.

The project aims to collect as little personal data as reasonably possible while still operating, improving, and securing the ecosystem. By using the Services, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Scope & Controller

This Policy applies to the official LUST Chain website, official explorer, public RPC endpoints, and ecosystem application hub operated under the LUST domains.

LUST Chain is an open-source, community-first project. Some infrastructure may also be operated by third parties outside core control. This Policy only covers Services directly controlled by the LUST Chain core team.

For the official Services, the practical data controller is the founder and the small core team coordinating infrastructure and development.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Emails, support messages, or direct contact requests.
  • Builder, contributor, partner, or collaborator applications.
  • Any information you choose to submit through official forms or communication channels.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and approximate region derived from IP.
  • Browser type, operating system, and basic device information.
  • Pages visited, referring URLs, request timing, and basic performance data.
  • Technical logs related to explorer usage, RPC requests, security, and uptime.

2.3 On-Chain Data

Transactions, wallet addresses, contract interactions, and related metadata on LUST Chain are public by design and may be indexed by anyone through explorers, nodes, or third-party analytics.

3. How We Use Data

We may use collected information to operate and maintain the Services, monitor uptime and security, detect abuse, improve infrastructure, review contributor applications, respond to support requests, and protect the ecosystem from malicious activity or technical disruption.

4. Cookies & Analytics

The Services may use cookies or similar technologies to remember basic preferences, support security controls, and measure aggregate usage.

Privacy-focused analytics tools may be used to understand ecosystem traffic and infrastructure performance while avoiding unnecessary collection of personal data.

5. On-Chain & Public Data

LUST Chain is a public blockchain. When you broadcast a transaction, your wallet address, amounts, contract calls, and related metadata become public and can be read, indexed, and analyzed by anyone.

Because blockchain history is generally immutable, the project cannot erase or rewrite confirmed on-chain data.

6. Sharing & Third Parties

The project may rely on third-party providers for hosting, analytics, delivery, monitoring, or infrastructure support. Wallets, bridges, exchanges, explorers, and dApps that interact with LUST Chain but are not official LUST Services may have separate terms and privacy policies.

Information may also be disclosed when reasonably necessary for security, legal compliance, or abuse prevention.

7. Storage, Security & Retention

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data under official project control. However, no system is perfectly secure, and you remain responsible for protecting your own wallets, devices, private keys, and seed phrases.

Operational logs and related off-chain data are retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for security, analytics, debugging, legal, or infrastructure purposes.

8. Your Rights & Choices

For off-chain information voluntarily provided to the project, you may request access, correction, or deletion where technically possible.

On-chain data cannot generally be modified or deleted because it is public and immutable by design.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under the age required by local law to provide valid consent to data processing. If you believe that a child has submitted personal information through official channels, contact the project so the matter can be reviewed.

10. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the project, infrastructure, legal obligations, or ecosystem structure. Continued use of the Services after an update means you accept the revised Policy.

11. Contact

  • Website: https://lustchain.org
  • Explorer: https://explorer.lustchain.org
  • Platform: https://platform.lustchain.org

Because LUST Chain is a community-driven project, response times may vary, but reasonable privacy-related requests will be reviewed in good faith.
