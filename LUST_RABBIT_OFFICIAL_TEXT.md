# LUST Rabbit Club - Official Text

## Main description

LUST Rabbit Club is the official founder NFT collection of LUST Chain.

10,000 premium Rabbits. One private founder club.

Your Rabbit is your identity, your membership pass and your early access badge inside the LUST ecosystem.

9,700 Rabbits are available for whitelist and public mint. 300 Rabbits are transparently reserved for community rewards, miners, ambassadors, ecosystem campaigns, partnerships and future LUST activations.

No rarity is secretly separated for the reserve. Traits, colors, accessories and rarity will appear after the official reveal.

## Benefits

Holders may receive:
- founder badge inside the LUST ecosystem
- private holder community access
- early access to selected LUST products
- discounts or priority in future NFT tools
- future holder campaigns
- eligibility for future companion activations such as LUST Bunny Pets or Rabbit Serum
- commercial use of the specific Rabbit NFT owned by their wallet, without claiming ownership of the official LUST Chain brand or trademarks
