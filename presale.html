Transparent rules

Either liquidity, or refund.

The contract keeps the LUSDT until the sale ends. If the softcap is not reached, refund is available. If the softcap is reached but liquidity is not created during the grace period, refund is also available.

Liquidity first

100% net LUSDT to WLST/LUSDT.

The sale was created to form the first WLST/LUSDT pool. The 0.20% fee can only be withdrawn after liquidity has been created.

Bonus schedule

Early participants receive more.

Days 1–15 receive 20% bonus, days 16–30 receive 15%, and days 31–60 receive 10%. Bonus unlocks after liquidity in 3 stages: 6, 9 and 12 months.
