Transparent rules

Either liquidity, or refund.

The contract keeps the LUSDT until the sale ends. If the softcap is not reached, refund is available. If the softcap is reached but liquidity is not created during the 7-day grace period, refund is also available.

Liquidity first

100% net LUSDT to WLST/LUSDT.

The sale was created to form the first WLST/LUSDT pool. The 0.20% fee can only be withdrawn after liquidity has been created. LP tokens stay locked in the presale contract for 12 months.

Buyer limits

Min 10 · Max 500 LUSDT per wallet.

The minimum buy is 10 LUSDT and the maximum contribution per wallet is 500 LUSDT. This keeps the Genesis phase accessible and reduces unfair concentration before liquidity opens.

Bonus schedule

Early participants receive more.

Days 1–15 receive 20% bonus, days 16–30 receive 15%, and days 31–60 receive 10%. Bonus unlocks after liquidity in 3 stages: 6, 9 and 12 months.

Claim rules

Purchased LST unlocks after liquidity.

Buyers can claim their purchased LST only after the WLST/LUSDT liquidity is created. Bonus LST is separate and follows the 6/9/12 month unlock schedule.

Fair launch sequence

Mining and staking open after liquidity.

Public mining and staking remain locked for the community until the first WLST/LUSDT liquidity pool is created. Presale first, liquidity second, mining and staking after.
