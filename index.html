Why it matters

Designed for transparent launch sequencing.

  • Presale before liquidity: net LUSDT is reserved for WLST/LUSDT liquidity.
  • Creator vesting: 2M LST locked with 180-day cliff after launch.
  • Public mining later: opens only after liquidity and H3B safety checks.
  • Treasury visibility: community and reserve wallets are labeled on explorer.
