LUST V31H2I mining page updated for Windows Register / Active flow.

Changed only mining.html plus this note file.

The page now clearly says:
1. Download Windows H2I full ZIP.
2. Extract into a fresh folder.
3. Open REGISTER-MINER-WINDOWS.bat.
4. Connect MetaMask on LUST Chain.
5. Claim faucet gas if needed.
6. Click Register wallet and confirm in MetaMask.
7. Click Activate / Heartbeat and confirm in MetaMask.
8. Open START-LUST-MINER-WINDOWS.bat.
9. Check STATUS-LUST-MINER-WINDOWS.bat and confirm any block on RPC/explorer.
