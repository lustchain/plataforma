Rules

Reward logic.

The explorer may show native custom rewards as 0. Confirm by wallet balance/RPC.

No public miner

Official fallback

Official producer keeps the chain alive and receives 2 LST if it produces.

One public miner

Public first

The public miner has first chance. If official fallback produces, official receives 1.4 LST and the active public wallet receives 0.6 LST.

Two or more public miners

70% / 30%

Producer receives 1.4 LST; other active public wallets share 0.6 LST. Official wallets are excluded from passive 30%.
