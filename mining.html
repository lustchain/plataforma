Eligibility period

Why the miner waits about 64 blocks after Active

After the Active transaction is confirmed, the wallet is not placed into the public mining rotation instantly. It enters a short on-chain maturity period first.

What changes after Active

The wallet is confirmed on-chain and the miner may already show SYNCED / MINING ACTIVE. This means the node is online and ready, but the reward wallet starts competing only after the maturity period ends.

When it starts competing

The eligibility block is calculated as Active confirmation block + about 64 blocks. With normal 15-second blocks, this is usually around 15 to 20 minutes.

Why this protection exists

The delay protects the network from instant wallet rotation, spam registrations and unfair last-second entries. It also gives the network time to propagate the operator and keep the rotation stable.

Release flow after Presale: download miner → extract ZIP → double click 1-INICIAR-MINERACAO-LUST.bat → copy Operator Address → Register → Active → wait about 64 blocks → wallet enters the public mining rotation. Until release, downloads stay locked.
After Active

How to check mining

The miner must stay open. If Gap is 0, peers are connected and sync is false, the local node is synchronized. If the wallet is still inside the 64-block maturity period, it is ready but not yet competing.

Mining rewards and fairness

One wallet, one chance

Each eligible public reward wallet has one mining chance. More CPU/GPU does not multiply the chance, and registering repeatedly does not create extra chances.

Public rewards

The live status estimates the 70% producer share from blocks mined by your wallet. Final balance also includes shared rewards, fees and transfers, and must be verified on-chain.

Official rewards

PROD1 and PROD2 have no wallet privilege in FINAL9. Official producer rewards are directed to the Treasury path.

Reward verification is transparent: mined blocks, wallet balances and treasury accounting can be checked in the explorer and by the public RPC. The wallet balance is the final on-chain source of truth.
